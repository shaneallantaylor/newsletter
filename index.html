For reasons I'm not completely sure of, here is a list of things I thought about recently:
  • Even though it's cold out, the wind makes it sound like the trees are singing when walking in my neighborhood in the evening.
  • A mindset that frames events, interections, and connections as opportunities for growth leads to a more enjoyable, fulfilling life. (My mom taught me that.)
  • An alternate hook in a song is infinitely more enjoyable if it is used sparringly (or even just once). Patience heightens things in a powerful way. Take the intro from A.D.H.D. by Kendrick Lamar for example. The beat doesn't fully reveal itself until 1:11 into the song! Genuis.
  • My dear friend Shane Henise recently made clear to me a useful frame to consider when understanding communication: Impact vs. Intent.
